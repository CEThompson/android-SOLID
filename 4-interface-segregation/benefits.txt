High Level Benefits

1. Communicates what clients do with their services
2. Communicates what clients don't do with their services
3. Restricts what clients can do with their services
4. (Segregated interfaces) Allows segregated functionality if/when needed
	[Great for refactoring legacy code]

Specific to 2-big-callback

1. Explicit and clear dependencies
2. More readable code without empty methods
3. Easy to find all clients which are interested in specific messages
4. Safe and easy to add new message types in the future
